About Me

Hello, I’m Faith Chepkoech Birgen, a Mass Communication graduate with a growing passion for programming and technology. My journey began with a love for storytelling and creativity, which led me to pursue a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication. During my studies, I honed skills in communication, media production, and audience engagement.
However, as I delved deeper into the digital world, I became fascinated by the power of technology to bring ideas to life. This inspired me to learn programming, where I discovered the perfect blend of creativity and logic.
Today, I’m focused on combining my communication skills with technical expertise to create user-friendly and impactful digital experiences. I am particularly interested in web development and digital storytelling. Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring new media trends, traveling, and connecting with people from diverse backgrounds.
